Igor's Carrot Rice

Igor's Carrot Rice is the white rice with carrot recipe that used when i am cooking rice for serving with any other dish

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 1 median carrot
  • 2 garlics
  • 1 tb olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Cut and chope the garlic, put in a pan with the olive oil (don't light the stove)
  2. Grate all the carrot, put in the same pan
  3. Grind the pepper in the pan
  4. Turn on the stove and stir everything until the garlic and carrots are kind golden
  5. Add the 2 cups of white rice and the salt (as you like)
  6. Mix all together for a while and then add the water (The measure of water for white rice is always double the measure of rice, eg, 2 cups of rice = 4 cups of water)
  7. Let it cook until the water level reaches the contents of the pan, as soon as it reaches it, reduce the heat and leave the pan half covered
  8. When the water has completely evaporated, cover and let it rest for 5 minutes, then serve
