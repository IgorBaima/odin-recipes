Coleslaw Salad

The only consistent ingredient in coleslaw is raw cabbage, while other ingredients and dressings vary widely.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small white cabbage or Â½ large
  • Pepper
  • 4 carrots, peeled
  • 1 large red onion
  • 1/2 Pineapple
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 50g Mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Honey

Steps:

  1. Cut the cabbage, carrots, onion and pineapple
  2. Put the pepper, mustard, mayo and honey
  3. Mix!
